Lolita: a bedazzled heart

Summary:

Lolita suddenly woken from her death slumber digs her way up. She finds herself in a graveyard. In her fit of exploring she meets ghostgirl Lily. The two become fast friends. Their objective becomes escaping the graveyard, but the groundskeeper and her minions keep them from doing so.
